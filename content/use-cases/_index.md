## Healthcare Clinics

From pharmacies to dental and dermatology clinics, patient expectations are rising while staff time stays limited. We help clinics deliver faster, more consistent service with:

- **AI chatbots** for common questions and pre-visit triage
- **AI-powered appointment booking** that reduces no-shows
- **CRM automation** to follow up on treatments and renewals

## Law Firms

Legal teams lose time to intake backlogs and scattered case updates. We streamline client acquisition and retention with:

- **Smart intake workflows** that qualify new matters quickly
- **Automated reminders** for deadlines, payments, and next steps
- **Client status portals** that reduce inbound support load

## Restaurants & Hospitality

Busy service teams need to move fast and keep every guest informed. We enable:

- **Reservation automation** with instant confirmations
- **Guest feedback loops** that surface service gaps early
- **CRM-driven campaigns** for repeat visits and loyalty

## Gyms & Fitness Studios

Membership growth depends on consistent follow-up and retention. We support:

- **Lead nurturing flows** for tours and trials
- **Automated onboarding** with personalised training plans
- **Retention signals** that trigger save actions before churn
